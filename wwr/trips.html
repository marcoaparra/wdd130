Trips

Click here and lets set a day, the water is waiting for you.

Intense blue

Here is the first experience for those who are starting in this new way of life. Is like if the nature would like to train us and show us how can help us, step by step for harder things and and includes some difficulties like some and sporadical brave waves. After all, what is life without a little difficulties?

Falling faster!

Now that you have experience, the level has increased. The road of "Falling faster!" includes more difficulties as little falls, and be closer of a great fall in the middle of the way (perfect to take some photos). But soon, the way continues and includes rapids that will require attention and all your security equipment well adjusted!

Golden waters

Golden waters are for those with golden medals in this area. It requires experience, braveness and a guide that will be with you all time for lead to you and your team trough this rapids. Each one of your team will cover some basic requirements and other not so basic to be on board this adventure. Safety first!

Name of the area Description Difficulty
Intense blue For begginers who has not any experience in this. Easy
Falling faster! For those who already has some experience and want more adrenaline. Medium
Golden waters You need experience and already been participated at least ten times in "Falling faster!" (Verifiable on your participant card). A guide will be assigned to lead you and your team. Hard
